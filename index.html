Appeal Guidelines

Why was my page suspended?

Your page may have been flagged due to violations of Meta's Community Standards, copyright issues, or suspicious activity.

Creating an effective appeal

Watch the instructional video to learn how to create a compelling appeal that addresses the reasons for suspension.

Tips for a successful appeal

  • Be concise and specific in your explanation
  • Maintain a respectful and professional tone
  • Provide factual information and evidence
  • Ensure all required information is accurate

What happens after submission?

Our team will review your appeal within 24 hours. We'll notify you via email about the decision. If approved, your page will be reinstated immediately.
